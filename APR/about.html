About

Hi, I'm Zach Shapiro.

For the past three years - one as an undergrad and two after college - I worked as a beat reporter covering the Tampa Bay Buccaneers.

In 2017 I decided to return to school to improve as a journalist and explore other topics, though with the goal of returning to sports writing. Here I am now in the Philip Merrill College of Journalism at the University of Maryland.
