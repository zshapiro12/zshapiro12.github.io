May 11, 2016
A Closer Look Into Aguayo's Collegiate Career
Pewter Report
One of, if not the, boldest move in the last decade of the NFL draft was Tampa Bay's decision to trade up for a kicker in the second round in 2016. The rare move, met with mostly shock and criticism from fans, made for a necessary look into the collegiate career that made Roberto Aguayo the highest drafted kicker since 2005. For those intersted in how it all turned out, he was released after a disastrous rookie year plus one preseason game.
